Round the year, guest talks, seminars and workshops are organized wherein eminent personalities from the corporate world and renowned institutions are invited to share real life experiences with students. A good blend of theory and practice is experienced by students. Guest lecturers at a college are usually up-and-comers, experts or well-known in their field. Colleges ask guest speakers to the campus for a variety of reasons. A teacher might invite a successful alumnus to come to the classroom and speak with students about how they broke into their field.
Guest lecturers need not be limited to campus-based courses. College professors from distance learning and online courses may also incorporate guest speakers into the curriculum. For example, online professors at Penn State may invite a guest speaker to hold a weeklong ongoing chat with students. Although the students do not get the opportunity to hear them speak in person, they still benefit from the interaction.
